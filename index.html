Grand Theft Auto VI is the highly anticipated next installment in Rockstar Games' iconic action-adventure series. As the eighth main entry and sixteenth overall, the game invites players into a sprawling open world set in the fictional state of Leonida, heavily inspired by Florida, and its vibrant, Miami-like Vice City. Players will dive into the thrilling story of Jason Duval and Lucia Caminos, a dynamic criminal duo navigating the dangerous underworld and high-stakes drama of this sun-soaked playground.
